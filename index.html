CARRIBEAN STUDENTS SCHOLARSHIP FUND, INC
OFFERING SCHOLARSHIPS TO PROMISING HIGH SCHOOL GRADUATES AND/OR RETURNING UNDERGRADUATE STUDENTS OF CARIBBEAN HERITAGE

OUR MISSION

The Caribbean Students Scholarship Fund, Inc. (CSSF, Inc.) is designed to establish and maintain a fellowship rooted in traditional and historical Caribbean principles of using one's skills in service to the community. Students interested in obtaining a scholarship must be of Caribbean descent and reside in the Washington metropolitan area (Maryland, Virginia and the District of Columbia).
