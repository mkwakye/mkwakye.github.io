SCHOLARSHIPS

CSSF offers scholarships to students in every field of study.

Special scholarships offered by CSSF include:

The Keith and Berna Warner, Love In Action Scholarship

“We recognize the invaluable contribution the CSSF, Inc. has made to the academic development of our students of Caribbean heritage and wish to continue our support on a personal level. We think it is important to show our seven grandchildren and all others of the coming generation the importance of giving back to the community, and of maintaining and strengthening their link to their heritage. We trust that our example will inspire both scholarship recipients and their supporters to continue the tradition of giving and make it possible for an even greater number of students to benefit.”

Candidate for the Keith and Berna Warner Scholarship must demonstrate need.

West Indian American Military Members Association (WIAMMA) Scholarship

The WIAMMA Scholarship, sponsored by individuals of Caribbean descent, who have served or are serving in the United States Armed Forces, provides funds to any student whose parent(s) have served in the U.S. Armed Forces.

“One of WIAMMA’s goals is to make a difference in the Caribbean community. Our relationship with the Caribbean Students’ Scholarship Fund, (CSSF, Inc.) program is a testament of our commitment to that cause. Our experience in the U.S. Armed Forces has been the foundation for the success in the lives of our members, and we can only trust that our contributions to the Caribbean Students’ Scholarship Fund Inc. will be the foundation that impacts change in the lives of our recipients. We hope they will not only keep the American flag flying high but also those of the Caribbean as well. We look forward to staying united with the CSSF program for a very long time.”

The purpose of the West Indian-American Military Members Association is to promote awareness, increase understanding and recognize the contributions of West Indian-Americans to the United States military. This is done through the exchange of culture, promoting diversity through education and celebration. Membership is extended to all who were born in the West Indies, or of West Indian parentage, and/or are married to a West Indian, and have served in any branch of the US Milatary.
