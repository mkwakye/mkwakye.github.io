APPLICATIONS
WHO MAY APPLY

Current high school seniors and full-time undergraduate and graduate college students.

HOW TO APPLY
Complete the application form and return the application along with the required documentation to:
Caribbean Students Scholarship Fund, Inc.
PO Box 1229
Landover, MD 20785-1229
Attn: Scholarship Application

ELIGIBILITY REQUIREMENTS

  • Be of Caribbean heritage.
  • US Citizen or Legal Permanent Resident
  • Reside in the Washington Metropolitan area (Maryland, Virginia and the District of Columbia).
  • Posses at least a 2.5 cumulative grade point average on a 4.0 scale.
  • Demonstrate financial need (students must apply for federal financial aid using FAFSA).
  • Not a previous recipient.

Scholarship recipients will be selected by the Caribbean Students Scholarship Fund, Inc., Advisory Board based on the above criteria. Candidates must avail themselves to a face-to-face interview with the Board.


All applicants will be notified in writing approximately four (4) weeks after the submission deadline.


Awardees are required to attend the Awards Banquet and must avail themselves to support activities that benefit the Scholarship Fund.
